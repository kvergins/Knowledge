# Monitor Communications

…the process of ensuring the information needs of the project and its stakeholders are met. 

- Keeps information flowing at an optimal rate 
- Ensures stakeholders are receiving the information they need, none that they don’t 
- Determines if project events merit changes to communications strategies 
- Ensures right content… 
- Reaches the right audience… 
- Via the right channel…
- At the right time 

### Communications Monitoring Factors
Project performance Occurrence of risk events Project changes/revisions Other project processes 

---

**Inputs:** Project management plan, project documents, work performance data, EEFs, OPAs      
**Tools & Techniques:** Expert judgment, project management information system, interpersonal & team skills, meetings     
**Output:** Work performance information, change requests, project management plan updates, project documents updates
